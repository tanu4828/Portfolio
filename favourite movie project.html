My Favourite Movies:-

1) Super 30:

Super 30 is a biographical film on Ananad kumar, a mathematician from Patna, India, works his way through challenges towrads success before running the Super 30 programme for IIT aspirants in Patna.

2) 3-Idiots:

3 Idiots is a 2009 comedy-drama film about three college friends, Rancho (Aamir Khan), Farhan (R. Madhavan), and Raju (Sharman Joshi), who drive to Shimla to find Rancho.

3) 12th Fail:

12th Fail is a 2023 Indian movie based on the 2019 non-fiction book of the same name by Anurag Pathak. The movie is about the real-life story of Manoj Kumar Sharma, an IPS officer who overcame poverty to achieve his success.
